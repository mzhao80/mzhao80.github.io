Michael Zhao is a first-year student at Harvard University studying Applied Mathematics with a focus in Computer Science & Economics.

At Harvard, Michael is involved in Harvard Data Analytics Group, Harvard Undergraduate Council, and the Harvard Glee Club.

Michael previously attended Lynbrook High School in San Jose, CA. There, he was recognized as a U.S. Presidential Scholar, a Coca-Cola Scholar, and a two-time National Finalist in the USA Biology Olympiad.
